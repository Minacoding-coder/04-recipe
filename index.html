Chicken Biryani

Image credit: Shehal at English Wikipedia

Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 2.2lbs.
  • Rice - 750g.
  • Onion - 3 medium.
  • Tomatoes - 4 medium.
  • Potatoes - 250g.
  • Garlic paste - 2tbsp.
  • Ginger Paste - 2tbsp.
  • Plain yoghurt - 200g.
  • Cooking oil - 175ml.
  • Biryani mix packet.

Recipe:

  1. Fry chopped onions in hot oil until golden. Add chopped tomatoes and fry until oil separates.
  2. Add meat, garlic pase, ginger paste, yogurt, potatoes and biryani mix. Stir fry for 10 minutes.
  3. Add 2 cups water.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat until meat is tender.Then increase heat and stir fry until oil separates from gravy.
  5. Separately. In 3 litres of boiling water, add some salt and the rice. Boil rice until 85% done.
  6. Spread half the rice in a pot, pour the meat curry on top.Spread the rest of the rice on top of the curry.
  7. Cover pot and cook on low heat for 10 minutes until rice is fully cooked.
  8. Enjoy!!
